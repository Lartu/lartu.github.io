2021-05-25: Farmers' Market

I had been looking forward to this day for almost a week. I had found out a farmers' market gathered every tuesday a few blocks from home and I was very excited to visit them. The experience was, unfortunately, somewhat lackluster.
— M93R95 (JPEG) - view original (3221 KiB, JPG)
The place was well stocked, but there were very few stalls. I had been to some of these markets in the past and there were more, but maybe that's because of the pandemic.
One man got mad at me for taking pictures. I realize I should've asked in the first place, but my mind was somewhere else. In any case, that didn't give the market any extra points.
My original idea was to come to this market and buy the groceries I'd need for the whole week. However, my usual shops seem to stock the same stuff they sell here, and I like supporting the small stores I tend to go to, so I don't believe my shopping habits will change a bit. Which is a little disappointing because I was looking for something new.
— J75U74 (b&w version) (JPEG) - view original (2888 KiB, JPG)
The leek I started growing some days ago has grown a lot! We were quite surprised at this. I don't know the first thing about plants, so I was expecting it to grow at a much slower pace. Nature is amazing.
I also put on, for the first time, a pair of socks I'd bought in Australia about two years ago. They are decorated with rainbowy jelly beans. I love colorful socks. When I moved by myself, I left the plain-colored pairs behind, so now my feet are always clad in pretty patterns.
