The Tao of Mox

Introduction
...

"The Tao of" is a phrase that originates from ancient Chinese philosophy. "Tao" translates to "the Way" or "the Path."

"The Tao of Mox" is an attempt towards a framework for mutual support, individual growth, and collective achievement within a group. Its goal is to provide a structured yet flexible pathway to pursue shared ambitions, celebrate personal progress, and cultivate a culture of encouragement and accountability.

Steps of the Tao of Mox
...

1. Channel and Group Creation
...

First, we'll establish a dedicated Telegram channel. This channel will be our exclusive space to share daily goals and achievements, promoting a standardized format to maintain focus and consistency.

Alongside, we'll create a Telegram group. This group will serve as a platform to discuss our progress, challenges, and provide mutual support in a respectful and positive atmosphere, avoiding comparisons and fostering encouragement.

2. Sharing Goals and Achievements
...

Every day, we'll share our goals in a Standardized Goal and Achievement Format, at the beginning of our day or the previous night, and update the channel with the day's achievements at the end of the day. Sharing challenges faced and how they were handled is key for fostering learning and resilience.

3. Recognition and Support:
...

We'll nurture a culture of recognition and support, celebrating everyone's achievements no matter their size. Positive reactions (as in Telegram reactions) and words of encouragement are crucial to keep morale high and maintain a positive atmosphere. Should a member need advice or support, the group will be there to help.

4. Weekly Review
...

Each week, we'll have a review where members can share reflections on what worked, what didn't, and how they plan to adjust their goals for the upcoming week. This will be done over the group and results will be shared on the channel.

5. Periodic Summaries:
...

Weekly, monthly, bi-monthly, quarterly, and annually, we’ll take a moment to compile and share a summary of our achievements and learnings in the channel, following a standardized format reflecting achievements, challenges, and future goals.

Standardized Goal and Achievement Format
...

Goal Messages
...

Goal messages should follow the following three-section format:

  1. Today: This section captures the current date and any specific circumstances of the day, such as the time you woke up or any unique situation affecting your routine. For example:
    • Today I woke up at 16:00.
    • I'm feeling unwell.
    • I overslept.
  2. Will Do: A list of tasks and objectives you plan to accomplish throughout the day. These can range from personal errands, work-related tasks, to self-improvement activities. For example:
    • Go to the supermarket.
    • Wash my clothes.
    • Have dinner.
  3. (Optional) Would Like To Do If There's Time: Additional tasks or activities you'd like to tackle if time permits. These are not urgent or crucial but would be nice to complete. For example:
    • Go for a walk.
    • Wash the dishes.

Achievement Messages
...

Achievement messages should follow the following two-section format:

  • Done: Tasks that have been successfully completed.
  • Not Done: Tasks that were not completed, with a brief explanation of why they weren't accomplished.

Manifesto
...

  • The Tao of Mox is a safe and respectful space to share and celebrate our daily achievements and goals.
  • Each member will share their goals and achievements in a standardized format, promoting consistency and reflection.
  • We'll foster a culture of mutual recognition and support, celebrating every achievement, no matter how big or small.
  • Avoid comparisons and encourage a perspective of personal growth and collective support.
  • Weekly reviews are a chance to share reflections, adjust goals, and celebrate achievements together.
  • If a member faces challenges, the group will provide a space for advice, support, and creative problem-solving.
  • We’ll review and adjust The Tao of Mox framework periodically to ensure it remains effective and beneficial for all members.
  • Confidentiality and mutual respect are fundamental to maintaining a supportive and positive environment in The Tao of Mox.
  • Periodic summaries are essential for recognizing our progress, learning from challenges, and adjusting our goals and strategies for the future.
  • Each summary will reflect our achievements, challenges, and learnings, strengthening our understanding and appreciation for our individual and collective effort.
  • Annual summaries will celebrate our journey together, reflecting how we’ve grown and how we can continue supporting each other towards our goals.
