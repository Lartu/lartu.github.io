# The LartuNet Book Club

Welcome to the Lartunet Book Club! In this corner of the LartuNet, I list the books I've read, along with when I started and finished them.
It's sort of like Goodreads, but for my personal use.

The Lartunet Book Club was started on April 12th, 2026, as an effort to read more often. I find reading very relaxing, but I also find it
very hard to set time aside for it. When I do get to it, though, I find that it calms the constant creative anxiety I have and helps me focus.

I inaugurate the Book Club in hopes that it will help me fix that! Let me know if there are any books you think I should read!
