Apparently, Peru is one of the worst countries in the world when it comes to food security.
I did not know this before going there. I contracted salmonellosis and parasites. I have never been so sick in my life.
All the medical professionals told me that this is far too common there and that eating in Peru is extremely dangerous.
I would advise you against visiting the country, even though it is beautiful and its people are among the nicest I have ever met.
